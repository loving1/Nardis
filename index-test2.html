About Narda

Narda has been vegan for 2 years, being previously vegetarian for 5 years. She changed her eating habits 7 years ago when she moved from her homeland Ecuador after researching ingredients on the foods she was eating in the States. She realized she was eating "dead foods" that made her feel tired and also was stopping her from having a healthy body. In 2007 she joined a Raw Vegan potluck and fell in love with the "living foods" and the joy that they bring to the everyday life. Since then she has never gone back. - Change your life by changing the foods you eat !
